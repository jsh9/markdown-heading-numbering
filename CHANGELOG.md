## [0.1.1] - 2026-04-19

### Fixed

- Heading numbers without a trailing dot (e.g., `## 1 Overview` or
  `### 1.1 What`) were not recognized as existing numbering and were left as-is
  instead of being renumbered.

- Full diff:
  https://github.com/jsh9/markdown-heading-numbering/compare/0.1.0...0.1.1
